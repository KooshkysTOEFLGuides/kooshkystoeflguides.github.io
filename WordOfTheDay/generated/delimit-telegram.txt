🔹 Word: delimit
🔊 Primary pronunciation: /diːˈlɪmɪt/

🧠 Meaning 1
Part of speech: transitive verb
Pronunciation: /diːˈlɪmɪt/
Definition: to mark or establish the physical boundaries of an area

Examples:

1. A stone wall delimits the northern edge of the property.
2. The river helps delimit the border between the two regions.
3. Archaeologists used wooden posts to delimit the excavation site.

🧠 Meaning 2
Part of speech: transitive verb
Pronunciation: /diːˈlɪmɪt/
Definition: to clearly define the scope, range, or limits of an idea, subject, study, or activity

Examples:

1. The researchers carefully delimited the scope of their investigation.
2. The introduction delimits the period covered by the historical analysis.
3. Clear eligibility rules delimit the group of people included in the study.
