🔹 Word: ubiquitous
🔊 Primary pronunciation: /juːˈbɪkwətəs/

🧠 Meaning 1
Part of speech: adjective
Pronunciation: /juːˈbɪkwətəs/
Definition: present, found, or appearing almost everywhere, especially within a particular place, society, or situation

Examples:

1. Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
2. Plastic is now ubiquitous in the world's oceans.
3. Coffee shops are ubiquitous throughout the city.
