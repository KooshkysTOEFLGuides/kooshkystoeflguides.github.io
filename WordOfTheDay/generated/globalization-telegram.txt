🔹 Word: globalization
🔊 Primary pronunciation: /ˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃən/

🧠 Meaning 1
Part of speech: uncountable noun
Pronunciation: /ˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃən/
Definition: the process through which countries, economies, cultures, and people become increasingly connected and dependent on one another around the world

Examples:

1. Globalization has allowed companies to sell their products in markets around the world.
2. Advances in transportation and communication have accelerated globalization.
3. Critics argue that globalization can weaken local industries and traditions.
