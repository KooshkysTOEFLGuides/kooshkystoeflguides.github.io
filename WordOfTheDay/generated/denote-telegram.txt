🔹 Word: denote
🔊 Primary pronunciation: /dɪˈnoʊt/

🧠 Meaning 1
Part of speech: transitive verb
Pronunciation: /dɪˈnoʊt/
Definition: to have a particular meaning or to represent a particular idea, object, or category

Examples:

1. In mathematics, the symbol π denotes the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter.
2. The term renewable energy denotes energy obtained from sources that can naturally be replaced.
3. Different colors on the map denote different types of land use.

🧠 Meaning 2
Part of speech: transitive verb
Pronunciation: /dɪˈnoʊt/
Definition: to show or indicate that a particular condition, quality, or situation exists

Examples:

1. A sudden drop in temperature may denote an approaching storm.
2. In this grading system, a score above 90 denotes excellent performance.
3. The presence of these chemicals may denote contamination of the water supply.
